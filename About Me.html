Amobiojo Usman

Certified and Professional Data Analyst With Years of Experience.

Usman Amonbiojo is a driven Data Analyst and Technology Advocate with a strong foundation in data-driven problem solving, business intelligence, and digital transformation. He is a trained data analytics professional with hands-on experience in transforming raw data into actionable insights that support strategic decision-making, operational efficiency, and sustainable development, particularly within the Nigerian context.With academic training in Data Analytics and practical exposure to industry-recognized certifications—including Cisco Data Analytics and the 3MTT (3 Million Technical Talent) programme—Usman has developed strong competencies in data cleaning, exploratory data analysis, statistical reasoning, dashboard development, and data storytelling. He is proficient in leveraging analytical tools such as Excel, SQL, Power BI, and Python to interpret complex datasets and communicate insights clearly to both technical and non-technical stakeholders.

Usman’s project portfolio reflects a commitment to solving real-world and socially relevant problems. Notably, he has conducted data-driven research on waste management in Nigeria, analyzing trends, inefficiencies, and policy gaps to propose evidence-based recommendations for improved environmental sustainability and public-sector performance. He has also worked on sales and business performance analyses, translating transactional data into strategic insights that support revenue growth and operational optimization. Beyond technical expertise, Usman is deeply passionate about technology inclusion and capacity building at the grassroots level, particularly in Kwara State, Nigeria. He actively promotes digital literacy and advocates for the adoption of data and emerging technologies as tools for economic empowerment, youth development, and community transformation.

Professionally, Usman demonstrates strong strengths in analytical thinking, attention to detail, problem-solving, and structured communication. His interdisciplinary background enables him to bridge the gap between data, business needs, and policy objectives. He is equally comfortable working independently or collaboratively in cross-functional teams and thrives in environments that value innovation, learning, and impact. Usman Amonbiojo is committed to continuous professional development and seeks opportunities where he can apply data analytics, technology, and strategic insight to drive measurable impact across the public sector, private enterprises, and development- focused organizations.
